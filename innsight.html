Effortless Hotel Deals at Your Fingertips

I created a hotel web scraper app designed to make finding the best hotel deals easier than ever. The core idea behind this app is to streamline the process of searching for hotels by gathering real-time data directly from Booking.com. By automating this task, users can quickly access the most up-to-date information on hotel availability, pricing, and reviews, without having to manually search across multiple pages.

Role: Lead Developer
Timeline: January 2024 - February 2024
Tools: Python, Pandas, Playwright, Tkinter
