Track Your Taste of India

Embracing the rich tapestry of flavors that Indian cuisine offers, I created this Indian Food/Macro Tracker software to address the challenge of finding a specialized tool to track and explore the diverse world of Indian culinary delights. Fueled by my passion for Indian food and a desire for a more tailored experience, I designed this project as a dedicated food tracker for individuals with similar interests as me.

Unlike generic food tracking apps, the Indian food tracker is tailored to the specific requirements of tracking Indian meals, ensuring a more accurate and personalized tracking experience. Furthermore, by offering detailed insights into the nutritional content of Indian ingredients and dishes, users can make informed decisions about their dietary choices, promoting a healthier lifestyle.

This was my first ever computer science project. It was initially meant for my family and friends, but I have recently been considering about making this a full-stack application and deploying it online. Stay tuned!

Role: Lead Developer
Timeline: May 2020 - June 2020
Tools: Python, Tkinter, JSON
